About Us

Who are we?
We are “Obvious Palace”. We formed our company with the goal of creating a game. Our motto is “simple and fun”.We wanted to create a simple run and gun like Contra or Cuphead that stood out. We thought a Run and Gun game being based around a fantasy setting with be a breath of fresh air in terms of theming in the genre and would be a simple and fun game to make. This game also has an unconventional control scheme which allows the player to fire and aim independently of movement in 8 directions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s different?
This game is a fantasy-based rather than military or sci fi based like most run and guns. This game also has an unconventional control scheme which allows the player to fire and aim independently of movement in 8 directions.
What is the main focus?
The corrupted wizard is driven to exact their vengeance against the lineage of his former Paladin friend for sealing centuries ago. The wizard aims to invade Obvious Palace and kill the Paladin’s descendant.
Where does the game take place?
This game takes place in a fictional fantasy land called Obviolous. It consists of varying terrains with the Obvious Palace at the center.
What do I control?
The player takes control of a corrupted wizard on a quest for revenge. They have the ability to make the wizard freely move, jump, and shoot fireballs at a variety of enemies.
